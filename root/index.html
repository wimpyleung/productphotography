    Quartz Illumination Bracelet

  1. Hero Shot – The image of the bracelet with a background similar to the existing brand, shows the special shape of the bracelet and focus on the details and unique colour in each grain of crystal.
  2. Individual Shots – Three standard shots of the existing brand, 45 degrees, top shot and side shot. Use a stone as a stand for the other two images, showing contrast of a stone and the natural crystal.
  3. Scale Shot – Use a standard bracelet gift box the show the scale of the bracelet. Not putting the bracelet inside the box as usual, place the bracelet on the box to show the bracelet clearly.
  4. Creative shot – Use a glass bottle to ‘wear’ the bracelet instead of a human model. Take this shot under sunshine with the glass bottle to make the crystal bracelet shine. Shoot in front of the trees and catch the shadow for background. Use natural elements - sunshine and tree shadow to show the characteristic of the natural crystal bracelet.
